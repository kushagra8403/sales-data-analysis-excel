Sales Data Analysis (Excel Dashboard Project)
Business Problem

A retail dataset was analysed to understand sales performance, identify trends over time, and determine key revenue drivers across products and regions.

Tools Used
Excel (PivotTables, PivotCharts, dashboards)
Data Cleaning & Transformation
Data Cleaning
Removed duplicates
Standardised date formats
Ensured numerical consistency for sales data
Checked for missing values
Analysis Performed
Monthly and yearly sales trend analysis
Top-performing products by revenue
Sales performance by region and category
Key Insights
Sales show a consistent upward trend from 2015 to 2018
Revenue peaks towards the end of each year, indicating seasonality
Identified top-performing products contributing the highest revenue
Detected fluctuations in monthly sales suggesting demand variation
